We found that we didn’t get time to speak to each other at dancing, as we were quite happy on the dance floor. This lead to us creating a “Nails and Wine night”, where we would catch up about dance, paint our nails and drink wine. Amongst us, we have some very creative nail artists (all amateur but enthusiastic).
